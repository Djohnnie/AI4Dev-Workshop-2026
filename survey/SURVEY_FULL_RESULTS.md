# Survey – Full Per-Participant Results

This document iterates through every question from [`SURVEY.md`](./SURVEY.md) and shows, in a single picture per question, **what each of the 28 participants answered**. The infographics are generated from [`SURVEY_RESULTS.md`](./SURVEY_RESULTS.md).

> **How to read the charts**
>
> - Each chart has 28 slots, one per participant (`#1` → `#28`).
> - Color coding follows the same dark-theme palette used in [`infographics/INFOGRAPHICS.md`](./infographics/INFOGRAPHICS.md): **red** = lowest / least mature, **orange** → **yellow** → **lime** → **green** = highest / most mature.
> - A muted grey stub (`—`) means the participant did not answer or selected *None of the above*.
> - For multi-choice questions the bar shows **how many options** that participant ticked (out of the maximum); the higher the bar, the broader their exposure / practice.
> - Open-text questions (Q10, Q19, Q20) are rendered as a 4×7 wall of cards with a truncated quote per participant.
