## Slide 02 — Chapter 3 — Speak AI's Language: Mastering Prompts, Workflow & Best Practices

![Slide 02 — Chapter 3 — Speak AI's Language: Mastering Prompts, Workflow & Best Practices](slide-302.svg)

> **TL;DR:** Chapter 3 merges prompting, context, workflow integration, and team best practices into one session.

In the one-day format, Chapters 5 and 7 from the two-day workshop are combined here. Participants will learn how Copilot reads context, how to write structured prompts with the right task, context, examples, and constraints, and how to build repeatable team habits around AI-assisted development. The session covers everything from the four-input prompt framework to conversation hygiene, token efficiency, and shared instruction files.
