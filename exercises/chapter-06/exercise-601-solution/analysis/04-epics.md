# Epics

## Epic 1 — Link creation

Enable a user to submit a long URL and receive a usable short link.

## Epic 2 — Link redirection

Enable visitors to open a short URL and reach the stored destination reliably.

## Epic 3 — Link statistics

Enable facilitators to inspect how often a link has been used and when it was last accessed.

## Epic 4 — Workshop operability

Keep the app easy to run, easy to verify, and easy to explain during the workshop.
